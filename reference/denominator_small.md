# denominator_small

Subset of the
[`denominator_data`](https://thomvolker.github.io/densityratio/reference/denominator_data.md)
with three variables and 100 observations

## Format

A data frame with 100 rows and 3 columns:

- x1:

  Continuous variable (mixture of normals with different means)

- x2:

  Continuous variable (normally distributed)

- x3:

  Continuous variable (normally distributed)
